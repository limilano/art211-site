Project 2: Video Collaboration

Initally for project 2 I was thinking of doing another dream but in a longer format. I was going to draw everything that I used in the video but during one of the first discussions we talked about only using found footage/images so I tried to do the same thing but with pictures.

After this we discussed as a class having a more limited source of footage and the loose theme of death in the digital age. My first video was kind of inspired by black box footage being shared on tv and online, and my second one was a play on LiveLeak which was a website where gore videos and military videos were shared in the 2010's. I thought it would be funny to edit together some dinosaur fighting/death clips like a prehistoric LiveLeak video.

I don't think I really had a strong idea for either of these pieces, I don't have a lot of experience with found footage so it was interesting to work with and find cool things to clip.

Wolf, Vostell. “Wolf Vostell - SUN in YOUR HEAD(1963).” Www.youtube.com, 1963, www.youtube.com/watch?v=VJaMHGIw188. Accessed 2 Apr. 2024. I really liked the high-contrast and flickering in his works, so I tried to mimic it a little bit without making it too hard to see what was going on.

“Custom Old Film Effect & Transition | after Effects Tutorial.” Www.youtube.com, www.youtube.com/watch?v=og3EcfVNSX0&t=426s. Accessed 2 Apr. 2024. I used this tutorial on the first 2 videos to try and create a more constant vintage effect across all the photos.
